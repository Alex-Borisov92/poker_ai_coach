# Micro MTT Stat Coaching

The player studies microstakes MTTs up to $15.

Useful coaching reads:

- VPIP/PFR gap above 5 points: check passive flats, limp calls, and missed iso raises.
- Low fold to 3Bet below 55 percent: check sticky 3bet defense, especially OOP versus tight ranges.
- High WTSD above 38 percent: review bluff-catchers, river calls, and medium-strength showdown hands.
- Low W$SD with high WTSD: likely calls too wide or arrives at showdown too weak.
- High 3Bet can be good if targeted versus steals. Verify by position and hand review.
- Good bb/100 does not remove leaks. Use it as baseline, then find EV improvement spots.

Always separate:

- Fact: exact stat and sample.
- Hypothesis: likely leak.
- Review action: hands or spots to inspect.
